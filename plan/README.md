## üí° Key Principles for Success

### 1. **Consistency Over Perfection**
Don't wait for perfect. Ship early, iterate fast.
- Publish blog posts even if they're not perfect
- Send that cold email even if you're nervous
- Launch features in MVP form

### 2. **Value First, Sales Second**
Always provide value before asking for anything.
- Help someone on Reddit ‚Üí They remember you
- Write helpful blog post ‚Üí They trust you
- Give free consultation ‚Üí They hire you

### 3. **Track Everything**
What gets measured gets improved.
- Log every lead source
- Track every conversion
- Measure every metric

### 4. **Focus on High-Impact Activities**
80% of results come from 20% of efforts.
- Blog posts > Random tweets
- Cold emails > Cold calls
- Case studies > Generic portfolio

### 5. **Build Systems, Not Hustle**
Systems scale, hustle doesn't.
- Create email templates (don't rewrite every time)
- Build CRM workflows (don't forget follow-ups)
- Automate repetitive tasks (save time)

### 6. **Learn from Every Client**
Every project is a learning opportunity.
- Ask for detailed feedback
- Document what worked
- Improve your process
- Build case studies

### 7. **Network with Intention**
Your network is your net worth.
- Connect with peers (collaboration)
- Connect with agencies (partnerships)
- Connect with clients (referrals)
- Give more than you take
