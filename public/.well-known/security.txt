# Security Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability in this portfolio or any of the 7K Ecosystem applications, please report it to us responsibly.

We take security seriously and appreciate your efforts to disclose your findings responsibly.

## Reporting Process

1. Email: kunalchheda13@gmail.com with [SECURITY] in the subject line
2. GitHub: Open a security advisory at https://github.com/kunu2009/7K-Portfolio-/security/advisories
3. Provide detailed information about the vulnerability
4. Allow reasonable time for us to respond before public disclosure

## What to Include

- Type of vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if you have one)

## Our Commitment

- We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- We will provide regular updates on our progress
- We will credit you for the discovery (unless you prefer to remain anonymous)

Thank you for helping keep 7K Ecosystem secure!
